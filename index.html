Hello!

I'm Daniel. I'm a research engineer, physicist, and biotechnologist. I want to understand the world, in the deepest possible sense, by connecting ideas across different fields.

At Datatonic, I worked directly with clients to implement end-to-end solutions on Google Cloud. This included developing an open source MLOps template in collaboration with Google Cloud. As a technical advisor for prospective customers, I also helped scope enterprise contracts.
